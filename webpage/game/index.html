Prison rules:
Avoiding solitary confinement
Time in solitary in confinement isn't tied to a person's sentence. Violating prison rules, some of them minor, can result in days or weeks in solitary. Think it's easy? Navigate the four scenarios below to see if you can avoid time in the box.
You have a medical dietary restriction.
You want to talk to a family member on the outside.
You want to read a book.
You want to exercise in the rec yard.
